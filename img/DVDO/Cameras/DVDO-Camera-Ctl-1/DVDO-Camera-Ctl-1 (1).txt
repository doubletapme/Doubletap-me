The DVDO Camera Control Module (CTL-1) is an intuitive and versatile device designed to provide seamless control over compatible DVDO cameras during video conferencing and live events. Ideal for professional meeting spaces, classrooms, and other collaborative environments, the CTL-1 enhances user control and customization, allowing for smooth camera operations with minimal effort.

Key Features of the DVDO Camera Control Module (CTL-1):
Precise PTZ Control: Offers full pan, tilt, and zoom (PTZ) control, enabling users to adjust the camera's position and zoom level for the perfect view, making it ideal for dynamic meetings or presentations.
Pre-Set Memory: Allows users to save and quickly access multiple camera presets, streamlining transitions between different viewpoints or angles.
User-Friendly Interface: Equipped with an intuitive control panel for easy operation, allowing quick adjustments during live sessions without disrupting the flow of the meeting.
Compact and Durable Design: Built to withstand frequent use in professional environments, with a compact form factor that fits comfortably on desktops or control stations.
Plug-and-Play Compatibility: Easily connects to DVDO cameras and integrates seamlessly with other conferencing systems, requiring minimal setup for immediate operation.
The DVDO Camera Control Module (CTL-1) is an essential accessory for organizations that prioritize professional-grade video conferencing experiences. By enhancing control over camera positioning and presets, it enables presenters and meeting facilitators to deliver a more polished and dynamic virtual experience.
